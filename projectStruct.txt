server :
	1 initialize the socket 
	2 create the socket : socket()
	3 bind the socket : bind()
	4 listen to the socket : listen()
	5 accept the connection :accept()
	6 send and recieve the connection : recv() send() recvfrom(), sendto()
	7 disconnect : closesocket()

client :
	1 initialize the socket 
	2 create the socket : socket()
	3 bind the socket : bind()
	4 send and recieve the connection : recv() send() recvfrom(), sendto()
	5 disconnect : closesocket()
